Slots Characteristics

This slot has three reels with a single payline. Each reel has 24 unique spaces. There are 7 symbols: Seven, Bell, 3-Bar, 2-Bar, 1-Bar, Cherry, and Slot Machine Symbol.

Payout of this slot machine when player bet 3 credits are as follows:

         Three Sevens: 600 Credits

         Three Bells: 300 Credits

         Three 3-Bar: 60 Credits

         Three 2-Bar: 45 Credits

         Three 1-Bar: 30 Credits

         Three Cherries: 6 Credits

         Two Cherries on the left and middle reel: 3 Credits

         Three of Any 3-Bar, 2-Bar, or 1-Bar: 1 Credits

         One Cherry on the left reel: 1 Credits

         Slot Machine Symbol on the left reel: 5 Bonus Free Games
